Hannibal's road to eat!🍖

Childhood🧒

Hannibal was born into a wealthy aristocratic family in Vilnius, Lithuania. He was the descendant of the warlord "Hannibal-the-grim" who defeated the Teutonic Order at the Battle of Grunwald and in five years built the castle called Castle Lecter.

During the winter of 1941 castle was sacked by Nazi Military forces which killed Lecter's family including his sister Mischa. Though helpless at that time he ran and hid himself with Soviet Forces and later on took revenge (you know how! 🥩) on each and every person who killed his family.

Hannibal and his tastes!😋

After becoming a psychiatrist, Hannibal quickly became a leading figure in Baltimore and indulged himself in extravagant tastes by financing from his wealthy clients/patients who he influenced them. He later became world-renowned as a brilliant psychiatrist. He also cursed psychology as a non-science field criticizing psychology department people as "ham radio enthusiasts and other personality-deficient buffs".😆

Hannibal became a serial killer after he was identified by Mason Verger who he nearly killed and was later convicted with at least nine murders and attempt to kill three others.

Hannibal was captured by his "Human" counterpart Will Graham in 1975 and assisted him in profiling several other murderers.🤠
