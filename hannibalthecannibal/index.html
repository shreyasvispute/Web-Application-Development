Dr. Hannibal Lecter💀

Biography

Hannibal Lecter is a successfull psychiatrist known for eating people with rude manners. Orphaned child who moved to America, notorious for eating😋 his victims, aka "Hannibal the Cannibal". Hannibal was also called as the Robin Hood of the killers. He has an eidetic memory with which he has constructed in his mind an elaborate memory palace with which he relives memories and sensations in rich detail.

Lecter's drawings earned him an internship in John Hopkins Medical Center in Baltimore, Maryland (not far from Stevens IT😨). He is a leading figure in Baltimore Society and is keen on indulging himself in extravagant tastes.
